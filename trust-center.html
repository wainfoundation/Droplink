Trust Center

At Droplink, we are committed to earning and maintaining your trust through robust security, privacy, and transparency practices. Learn how we protect your data and ensure compliance while providing a seamless link-in-bio experience integrated with the Pi Network.

1. Security Measures

We prioritize the security of your data with industry-standard practices, including:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS and TLS protocols.
  • Secure Storage: Your personal information and Pi Wallet ID are stored securely with restricted access.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and address risks.
  • Account Protection: We implement strong authentication mechanisms to protect your account from unauthorized access.

While we take every precaution, you are responsible for securing your account credentials and Pi Wallet ID.

2. Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is at the core of our operations. We adhere to the following principles:

  • We only collect data necessary to provide and improve the Service, as outlined in our Privacy Notice.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We provide transparency about how your data is used, including for Pi Network transactions (e.g., payments, tips).
  • You have control over your data, with options to access, correct, or delete it as described in our Privacy Notice.

3. Compliance

Droplink is committed to complying with applicable laws and regulations, including:

  • Data Protection Laws: We comply with privacy laws such as GDPR (for EU users) and CCPA (for California residents), ensuring your rights are protected.
  • Pi Network Guidelines: We adhere to Pi Network's policies for transactions and data handling involving Pi currency.
  • Industry Standards: We follow best practices for security and privacy, such as ISO 27001 principles for information security management.

We regularly review our practices to ensure ongoing compliance with evolving regulations.

4. Transparency

We believe in being transparent about our practices:

  • Our Terms & Conditions, Privacy Notice, and Cookie Notice provide detailed information about how we operate.
  • We notify users of significant changes to our policies or practices via email or notices on the Service.
  • We provide clear information about how Pi Network transactions are processed and how your Pi Wallet ID is used.

5. Third-Party Partnerships

We work with trusted third parties to provide the Service, including:

  • Pi Network: For processing payments and transactions using Pi currency.
  • Service Providers: For hosting, analytics, and other operational needs, under strict confidentiality agreements.

We ensure that all partners meet our standards for security and privacy. However, we are not responsible for the practices of third parties like Pi Network, and we encourage you to review their policies.

6. Reporting Issues

If you identify a security vulnerability or have concerns about your data, please report it immediately:

  • Email: security@droplink.com
  • For general inquiries, visit our Contact Page.

We will investigate and address all reported issues promptly.

7. Contact Us

For questions about our Trust Center or any of our practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@droplink.com
  • Visit our Contact Page
