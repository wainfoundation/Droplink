Cookie Notice

Last Updated: April 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Droplink uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our link-in-bio platform, integrated with the Pi Network. This Cookie Notice explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences. By using Droplink ("Service"), you consent to our use of cookies as described in this notice.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember your preferences, improving your browsing experience. We also use similar technologies like local storage and pixel tags.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Service to function, such as maintaining your login session and enabling Pi Network transactions (e.g., for paid plans or tips).
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data about how you use the Service (e.g., pages visited, time spent) to help us improve its performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as your chosen template or language settings, to provide a personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Track usage patterns to understand user behavior and optimize the Service (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns, often set by third-party advertising partners.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the Service works properly, including secure logins and Pi Network payment processing.
  • Analyze usage data to improve the Service and fix issues.
  • Personalize your experience by remembering your settings and preferences.
  • Provide relevant ads and measure their effectiveness (if applicable).

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party cookies for analytics and marketing purposes, such as:

  • Google Analytics: To track usage and improve the Service.
  • Advertising Partners: To deliver targeted ads (if applicable).

Third-party cookies are subject to the privacy policies of those providers. We do not control their practices.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences in the following ways:

  • Cookie Settings: Adjust your preferences via our Cookie Preferences page, where you can opt out of non-essential cookies.
  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of the Service.
  • Do Not Track: We respect "Do Not Track" signals if enabled in your browser, limiting tracking for non-essential purposes.

For more information on cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

7. Changes to This Cookie Notice

We may update this Cookie Notice from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via email or through a notice on the Service. Your continued use of Droplink after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated notice.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Cookie Notice or our use of cookies, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@droplink.com
  • Visit our Contact Page
